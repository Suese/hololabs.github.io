The Farrago AR FunKit is an easy-to-use augmented reality creator for iOS that lets you add 2-D and 3-D special effects to your photos and videos in real time.

Features

  • Farrago comes loaded with a library of 2-D and 3-D objects you can easily arrange within the view screen of your camera. These objects can be rotated, resized and retouched for maximum Farrago-ness.
  • Farrago also features a user-friendly editor that lets you to create your own objects from photos.
  • Farrago is compatible with augmented reality markers, printable patterns that allow your models greater freedom of movement. An object associated with a marker will appear within the camera's view screen. For example, someone holding a marker associated with a laser-gun will appear to be holding a laser-gun through the camera's view screen. To use this feature, you can print out markers within the app or download markers from our website.
  • Easily share photos and videos online with one-touch publishing.
