FAQ

What is Papercade?
Papercade (iOS/Android) is a creative, social game where players can easily craft together story games. Assemble pictures and ‘paper’ cutouts in a virtual shoebox to create dioramas for and about your friends.

How does Papercade work?
Papercade is a mobile game that combines story telling with simple game creation. Players take photos from their device or social networks to create personalised story games for and about their friends. Players can play other games created by members of the Papercade community.

How did you come up with the idea to make Papercade?
Hololabs co-founders Paul Warne and Mike Wozniewski came up with the idea for Papercade because they wanted to create a way for anyone, even the average mobile user, to build their own games and stories. Paul has over 15 years of AAA game development experience, and Mike has a background in developing tools for interactive display artists.

Is scrap-gaming a whole new type of gameplay?
Papercade’s look and feel throwback to making shoebox dioramas in grade school with photographs, paper cutouts, and found materials. We call it “scrap-gaming” to accurately describe the experience a player has while they are creating a story game in Papercade.

What is your revenue model?
Papercade is a free-to-play game with in-app payments.

When can I play Papercade?
Coming soon to a device near you! Sign up for updates at www.papercade.com.

How can I get involved with Papercade now?
Get in touch! We are always looking for creative people interested in trying out the early versions of Papercade. Sign up on the website or send us an email and get your creator hat on!

How can I stay up-to-date with Papercade’s development?
Sign up for updates on www.papercade.com, and join us on Facebook, Twitter, Google+, and Pinterest.

Who is the team behind Papercade?
Papercade is a Hololabs product. Hololabs is based in Montreal, Canada. You can learn all about us here .

We’re at GDC! Drop us a line if you want to connect and meet up. We will have a big surprise for everyone attending PAX East in April so stay tuned!

What else does Hololabs do besides Papercade?
Papercade is our first game, but we are working on other projects right now as well. In April we are collaborating with recording artist Kid Koala in celebration of Norman McLaren’s 100th birthday with the National Film Board.

How did Hololabs start?
From live multi-city video game installations to augmented reality mobile apps, Hololabs started developing new technologies to better serve creativity in 2011. Mike Wozniewski and Paul Warne started Hololabs to explore imaginative and meaningful ways for people to connect through games and new media.
