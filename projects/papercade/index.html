FAQ

What is Papercade?
Papercade (iOS) is a free, creative, social game where players can easily craft together storygames. Assemble pictures and paper cutouts in a digital shoebox to create dioramas for and about your friends.

What is a storygame?
A 'storygame' is our own creation. Combine your photos with our cutouts and make any story you want to tell. Make it easy, or make it challenging. Make it funny, or serious. They are your stories and we want to make it simple to be as creative as possible.

How does Papercade work?
Papercade is a mobile game that combines storytelling with straight-forward game creation. Players take photos from their device to create personalized storygames for and about their friends. A simple storygame can be made in a matter of minutes, or players can create elaborate dioramas and truly make it their own. Once complete the Papercade can be published to a social feed for the create community to play.

We're looking forward to seeing yours!

How did you come up with the idea to make Papercade?
Hololabs co-founders Paul Warne and Mike Wozniewski came up with the idea for Papercade with the aim of creating a way for anyone, from the hardcore gamer to the average mobile user, to build their own games and stories. Paul has over 15 years of AAA game development experience, and Mike has a background in developing tools for interactive displays for artists.

What makes Papercade difference from other mobile games?
Papercade's look and feel throwback to making shoebox dioramas in grade school with photographs, paper cutouts, and found materials. We call it 'scrapgaming' to describe the experience a player has while creating dioramas in Papercade.

What is your revenue model?
Papercade is a free-to-play game with in-app purchases.
